The recipes in the cookbook range from simple tasks, such as manipulating strings and validating dates in JavaScript, to entire libraries that demonstrate complex tasks, such as cross-browser positioning of HTML elements and draggable elements. The book contains over 150 recipes on the following topics: managing browser windows and multiple frames; working with forms and style sheets; supporting various kinds of page navigation; producing visual effects for stationary content; positioning HTML elements; and creating dynamic content.
